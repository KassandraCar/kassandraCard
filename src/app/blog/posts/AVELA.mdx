## About AVELA

AVELA (A Vision for Engineering Literacy & Access) is a student-led organization dedicated to expanding access to engineering education and opportunity.  

Our work centers on increasing visibility, literacy, and belonging within STEM spaces — particularly for students historically underrepresented in engineering.

Through outreach, programming, and institutional collaboration, AVELA aims to reduce systemic barriers and build sustained pathways into technical fields.

---

## What We Do

AVELA operates at the intersection of education, mentorship, and access. Our initiatives include:

- Hosting scholarship nights and graduate school information sessions  
- Coordinating speaker series focused on representation in engineering  
- Partnering with K–12 schools across Washington for STEM outreach  
- Supporting students navigating professional and academic pathways  
- Facilitating conversations around equity and inclusion in technical spaces  

We believe engineering literacy is not just about technical knowledge — it is about understanding systems, opportunity structures, and long-term career pathways.

---

## Leadership & Impact

As Co-President of AVELA, I help coordinate:

- Board operations and strategic planning  
- Institutional partnerships  
- Budget management and funding proposals  
- Event logistics and speaker outreach  
- Community engagement across high schools and university networks  

AVELA has engaged hundreds of students annually through outreach programs, speaker events, and mentorship initiatives.

Our goal is not only to inspire interest in engineering — but to ensure students have the structural support to persist and thrive.

---

## Why This Work Matters

Access to engineering education remains unequal. Many students lack:

- Clear information about scholarships and fellowships  
- Exposure to diverse engineering role models  
- Institutional mentorship networks  
- Familiarity with graduate pathways  

AVELA works to address these gaps by creating spaces where students can ask questions, build confidence, and access tangible resources.

---

## Looking Forward

AVELA continues to expand partnerships across Washington, strengthen student leadership development, and build programming that reflects the needs of our community.

The long-term vision is sustainable access — ensuring that engineering spaces are not exclusive, but inclusive by design.
